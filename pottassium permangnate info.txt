Pemayangtse Monastery – A Spiritual & Cultural
Gem of Sikkim
Short Description
Pemayangtse Monastery, one of the oldest and most important monasteries in Sikkim, lies
about 8 km from Pelling. Belonging to the Nyingma order of Tibetan Buddhism, it was
founded in 1705 by Lama Lhatsün Chempo. The monastery is renowned for its exquisite
architecture, colorful murals, ancient relics, and its annual festivals featuring masked
Cham dances. It serves as a key spiritual center for Sikkimese Buddhists.
